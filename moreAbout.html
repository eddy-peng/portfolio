Art History

I'm an Art Historian at heart!

I discovered my love of Art History when I was a Freshman in college. Art History made me realize that every detail has meaning and should be appreciated. Nighthawks has got to be my all-time favorite painting! Nighthawks resonates with me because it depicts America in a time of uncertainty and anxiety, two common feelings in this day and age.

Edward Hopper, Nighthawks, 1942. Oil on Canvas. 33.1 in x 60.0 in. Art Institute of Chicago.
