I have always been interested in business, research, psychology, and design. I saw UX Design as a middle ground for all my interests.

Respectfulness towards peers and their ideas

Everyone has their perspectives, experiences, and ideas. However, we can't read each other's minds to determine their thoughts. Keeping this in mind, I give other peoples' proposals the time to shine by listening and admiring them for their experiences.

It's okay to make mistakes

We humans often make mistakes since it's in our nature. However, it is okay. Making mistakes allows us to learn, make them part of our experience, and reflect on ways to prevent them from reoccurring later on.

I have much to learn

As a college student, I understand there are areas of expertise where I have little to no knowledge. That will be true for me even after I graduate. With UX design rapidly evolving as a field, there will always be room for growth. By keeping an open mind, I can learn from fellow peers and mentors without the fear of feeling insecure.
